## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do you offer consultations?

Yes! Every service begins with a complimentary consultation to understand your vision, face shape, and lifestyle.

### Is Luxe Studio for men and women?

Absolutely. We are a unisex salon offering premium grooming for all genders.

### Do I need to book in advance?

We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability with your preferred stylist, but walk-ins are welcome on weekdays.

### What should I bring to my appointment?

Just yourself! If you have inspiration photos, we'd love to see them during your consultation.

### Do you offer bridal or event styling?

Yes, we offer special occasion styling. Please call us at +1 (212) 555-0199 to discuss your needs and book a trial session.

### What products do you use?

We use premium professional-grade products selected for quality and results. Our stylists can recommend products tailored to your hair type.
